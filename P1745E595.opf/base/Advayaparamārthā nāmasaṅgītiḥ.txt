kulatrayaḥ

sarvamantrārthajanako mahābinduranakṣaraḥ|pañcākṣaro mahāśūnyo binduśūnyaḥ ṣaḍakṣaraḥ

sarvākāro nirākāraḥ ṣoḍaśārdhārdhabindudhṛk|akalaḥ kalanātīścaturthadhyānakoṭidhṛk

sarvadhyānakalābhijñaḥ samādhikulagotravit |samādhikāyaḥ kāyāgyraḥ sarvasaṃbhogakāyarāṭ

nirmāṇakāyaḥ kāyāgyro buddhanirmāṇavaṃśadhṛk|daśadigviśvanirmāṇo yathāvajjagadarthakṛt

devātidevo devendraḥ surendro dānavādhipaḥ|amarendraḥ suraguruḥ pramathaḥ pramatheśvaraḥ

uttīrṇabhavakāntāra ekaḥ śāstā jagadguruḥ|prakhyātadaśadigloko dharmadānapatirmahān

maitrīsannāhasannaddhaḥ karuṇāvarmavarmitaḥ|prajñākhaḍgo dhanurbāṇaḥ kleśājñānaraṇañjahaḥ

mārārirmārajidvīraścaturmārabhayāntakṛt|sarvamāracamūjetā sambaddho lokanāyakaḥ

vandyaḥ pūjyo'bhivādyaśca mānanīyaśca nityaśaḥ|arcanīyatamo mānyo namasyaḥ paramo guruḥ

trailokyaikakramagatirvyomaparyyantavikramaḥ|traividyaḥ śrotriyaḥ pūtaḥ ṣaḍabhijñaḥ ṣaḍanusmṛtiḥ

bodhisattvo mahāsattvo lokātīto maharddhikaḥ|prajñāpāramitāniṣṭhaḥ prajñātattvatvamāgataḥ

ātmavitparavitsarvaḥ sarvīyo hyagrapudgalaḥ|sarvopamāmatikrānto jñeyo jñānādhipaḥ paraḥ

dharmadānapatiḥ śreṣṭhaścaturmudrārthadeśakaḥ|paryupāsyatamo jagatāṃ niryāṇatrayayāyinām

paramārthaviśuddhaśrīstrailokyasubhago mahān|sarvasampatkaraḥ śrīmān mañjuśrīḥ śrīmatāṃvaraḥ

iti kṛtyānuṣṭhānajñānagāthāḥ pañcadaśa|namaste varadavajrāgyra bhūtakoṭe namo'stu te|namaste
śūnyatāgarbha buddhabodhe namostu te

buddharāga namaste'stu buddhakāma namo namaḥ|buddhaprīte namastubhyaṃ buddhamoda namo namaḥ

buddhasmita namastubhyaṃ buddhahāsa namo namaḥ|buddhavāca namastubhyaṃ buddhabhāva namo namaḥ

abhavodbhava namastubhyaṃ namaste buddhasaṃbhava|gaganodbhava namastubhyaṃ namaste jñānasaṃbhava

māyājāla namastubhyaṃ namaste buddhanāṭaka|namaste sarvasarvebhyo jñānakāya namo'stu te
